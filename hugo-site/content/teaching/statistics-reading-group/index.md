The Sociology Statistics Reading Group was an approximately bi-weekly group for discussing statistical methods papers drawn from a wide range of literatures. Each meeting centered on a paper or small set of papers, with one discussant walking through the material before a broader group discussion. The presentations below often draw figures from the original papers and, in some cases, from presentations generously shared by the paper authors.
